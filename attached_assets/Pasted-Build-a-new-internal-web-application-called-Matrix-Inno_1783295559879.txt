Build a new internal web application called Matrix Innovation Hub.

Goal:
Create v0.1.0 of an internal Matrix Innovation Hub application used to collect, evaluate, score and manage AI innovation initiatives.

Use:
- React
- TypeScript
- TailwindCSS

v0.1.0

- Risks

Scoring model:
Use a 100-point scoring model:
- Business Value: 25
- Revenue Potential: 15
- Cost Savings: 15
- Customer Impact: 15
- Strategic Alignment: 10
- AI Readiness: 10
- Prototype Confidence: 10

Also subtract:
- Technical Complexity penalty: 0 to -10
- Risk penalty: 0 to -10

Priority:
- 80+ = Critical
- 65–79 = High
- 50–64 = Medium
- Below 50 = Low
